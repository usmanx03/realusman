Backend work that is doing its job tends to be quiet. Fast responses, consistent data, graceful failure handling. That is the standard I hold things to.

I approach problems at the system level: component boundaries, data flow, failure modes, operational cost. Production behaviour matters as much as correctness on paper.

Proven tools over interesting ones. Straightforward solutions over layered abstractions. The goal is a system the next engineer can understand and operate.
